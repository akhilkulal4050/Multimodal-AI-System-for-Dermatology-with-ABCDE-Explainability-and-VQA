Q: What is melanoma?
A: Melanoma is a malignant tumour of melanocytes, the pigment-producing cells of the skin. It is the most lethal form of skin cancer, responsible for over 57,000 deaths annually worldwide. Risk factors include UV exposure, fair skin (Fitzpatrick I-II), family history, BRAF/NRAS mutations, and the presence of multiple atypical nevi.
