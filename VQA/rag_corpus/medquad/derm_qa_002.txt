Q: What are the ABCDE criteria for melanoma?
A: ABCDE criteria: Asymmetry (one half does not mirror the other), Border irregularity (ragged, notched, or blurred edges), Color variation (multiple shades of brown, black, red, white, or blue-grey within one lesion), Diameter greater than 6mm, Evolution (any change in size, shape, color, or new symptoms such as bleeding or itching).
