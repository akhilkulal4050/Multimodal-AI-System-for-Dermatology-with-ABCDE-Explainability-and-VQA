Q: What is the ABCD rule of dermoscopy?
A: The dermoscopic ABCD rule scores: Asymmetry 0-2 points (two perpendicular axes), Border abruptness 0-8 points (8 segments), Color richness 1-6 points (up to 6 dermoscopic colors), Dermoscopic structures 1-5 points (network, dots, streaks, regression). Total Dermoscopic Score = (A×1.3)+(B×0.1)+(C×0.5)+(D×0.5). TDS <4.75 = benign, 4.75-5.45 = suspicious, >5.45 = highly suspicious for melanoma.
