Q: How is basal cell carcinoma treated?
A: BCC treatments: surgical excision (95% cure rate), Mohs micrographic surgery (98-99% cure rate for high-risk/recurrent BCC), electrodesiccation and curettage for small superficial lesions, cryotherapy, topical imiquimod or 5-fluorouracil for superficial BCC, radiation therapy, and vismodegib/sonidegib (hedgehog pathway inhibitors) for locally advanced or metastatic BCC.
