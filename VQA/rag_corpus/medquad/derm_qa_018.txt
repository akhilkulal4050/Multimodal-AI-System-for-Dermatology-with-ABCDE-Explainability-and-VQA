Q: What is a melanocytic nevus?
A: A melanocytic nevus (common mole) is a benign proliferation of melanocytes. Types: junctional (flat, at dermal-epidermal junction), compound (slightly raised, both junctional and dermal), intradermal (raised, dome-shaped, predominantly dermal). Most nevi are stable. Atypical (dysplastic) nevi have irregular features and increased melanoma risk.
