Q: What is Mohs micrographic surgery?
A: Mohs surgery removes skin cancer in horizontal layers with immediate intraoperative microscopic margin assessment. Each layer is mapped; removal continues only where tumour remains. Achieves highest cure rates: 98-99% for BCC, 92-97% for SCC. Ideal for tumours on face, ears, eyelids, nose, and lips where tissue conservation is critical.
